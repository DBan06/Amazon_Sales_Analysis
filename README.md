# Amazon_Sales_Analysis

![image](https://github.com/DBan06/Amazon_Sales_Analysis/assets/82170668/7c4f65f8-2554-440d-8652-b62fa67b0a93)

Data Used
Data - Amazon Sales data for 4 consecutive years from 2019 to 2022.

Data Cleaning & Analysis - Power BI
1) First we imported the raw xlsx into our power BI desktop
2) Created custom column to separately identify the year column.
3) Created custom column to identify the delivery days taken for each item to get delivered. Here we subtracted the order date from the shiped date.
4) Created condition condition column to identify if the items were returned or not.

Data Visualization - PowerBI

Summary of Findings
1) With the help of the dashboard we were able to visualize the sales trend of Amazon for 2019,2020,2021 and 2022 both individually and as a whole for 4 years.
2) We are able to identify the total sales, total products sold, average days taken for items to get delivered and also the total returns.
3) We found out that the sales for consumers is significantly more as compared to corporate and home office.
4) We were also able to identify that the sales in Asia Pacific region is maximum closely followed by Europe.
5) We were able to identify the top 5 and bottom 5 products in terms of profit. The item canon imageClass2200 advanced copper is the most profitable item where as cubify cubex 3D double head printer is the least profitable product.
6) We were also able to identify top 10 most profitable.Tamara Chand is the most profitable customer.
